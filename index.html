EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK (ABRIDGED)

Sanity is your health. At zero, you burn out and the run ends.
Time resets each morning. Run out and you enter Crunch: sanity costs rise 50%.
Credit is what your work is worth. Every result you produce appears on the At Risk tracker (top bar, and in full at end of day).
Paper Trails lock an item so it cannot be claimed. Locks cost Time. Targets freeze at midnight Thursday.
Friday, 4:00 PM: Director Miller reads the week aloud and claims your most valuable unlocked item. Later weeks, he claims more. Lock everything and he claims nothing.
Reputation matters at the edges. Below 30, you may not decline evening "opportunities." At 70 or above, your standing shields your single best unlocked item from being claimed (he cannot publicly take the headline win of someone that visible).
PTO skips a card. Its use is noted.
Leverage is paid at week's end and spent on Saturdays, on the version of you that leaves. Three purchases make up the Escape Plan. Complete it.

This handbook is provided for your convenience. It is not a contract. Nothing is a contract.
