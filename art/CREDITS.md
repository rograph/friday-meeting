# Art Credits

## Approved sources
- [Kenney.nl](https://kenney.nl/assets) — CC0, primary source for placeholder/base sprites and UI.
- [Piskel](https://www.piskelapp.com/) — free browser tool for custom pixel art (not an asset source, a tool).
- [Krita](https://krita.org/) — free/open-source tool for custom art (not an asset source, a tool).
- [OpenGameArt.org](https://opengameart.org/) — filter to CC0/CC-BY only.
